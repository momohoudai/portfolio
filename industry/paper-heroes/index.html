Paper Heroes

Paper Heroes is a Android game written with Unity by UDream Entertainment. It is a lightweight, casual and idle strategy game, where you form your team of paper-styled pirates and battle against other crews

The game is currently avaliable globally on Play Store.

In this project, I worked as a backend engineer. My responsibilities include the backend architecture of the game, which includes server-side gameplay code, build/deployment pipeline, structuring databases and determining the data pipeline for business analytics.
