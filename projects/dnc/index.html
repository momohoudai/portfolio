Dots and Circles

Be a Dot! Be a Circle! Be anything you want, just avoid what you are not!

Dots and Circles is an experimental Bullet Hell game that uses mouse as movement. Collect as many bullets as you can to see how much you can score!

  • Move around with the mouse
  • Left click to change between being a Dot or a Circle.

This game and its engine is written from scratch with the ‘handmade’ philosophy. Almost everything is coded from ground up in C/C++ for reasons mostly educational. The only code not written by me are therenderer APIs, OS APIs, a couple of stb and standard library headers.
