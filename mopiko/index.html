Jan 1, 0001
Momoko Moko was born in Gridania to a working traditional family of two Lalafells: her mother is an archer and her father is a tanner. Since young, Momo is a distracted girl with a strong sense of wonder. When she was young, Momo was found to have an affinity for earth, water and wind, immediately attracting the notice of the conjurer’s guild who took her in. The tdlr is that Momo grew to be a powerful white mage, but is unconventional, difficult to predict and hard to get a hold of, which is something the conjurers as a whole had to endure.
